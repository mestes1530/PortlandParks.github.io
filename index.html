Peninsula Park

Link To Location 700 N Rosa Parks Way

The historic Peninsula Park is home to the cities oldest public rose garden and second oldest playground. It also features a bandstand that is used for concerts and various meet ups.

Unthank Park

Link To Location Denorval Unthank City Park

Named after Dr. DeNorval Unthank, Unthank park has multiple basketball courts as well as fields for grass sports.



Arbor Lodge Park

Link To Location N Delaware Ave & North Bryant Street

From the playground build into the parks lanscaping to tenis courts and baseball fields, Arbor Lodge Park has the activities for the whole family in a relativly small location.

Overlook Park

Link To Location 1599 N Fremont St

A park with an amaizng view of downtown Portland as well as the North-West industial area. The park is dog-freindly and has varios courts and fields for all types of sports.

Gammans Park

Link To Location N Gammans Pl

With a small playground and a narrow pathed bike path, Gammans Park offers a central meeting space for those in the surronding North-West Portland neighborhood.

Columbia Park & Annex

Link To Location 4503 N Lombard St

A large wooded park with multiple full sized fields, overhangs, a playground and a public indoor pool. Columbia Park & Annex truely has something for everyone.


Dawson Park

Link To Location 1 N Stanton St

Located right outside the Legacy Emanuel Medical Center, Dawson Park is a small park with playgrounds for kids and seating for those looking to get fresh air.


Kenton City Park

Link To Location 8417 N Brandon Ave

Recently renovated in 2018, Kenton City Park has state of the line playground and newly installed picnic tables. A great short visit for anyone shopping in Kenton.
